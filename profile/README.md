<h2 id="about">About</h2>

<p>
<strong>Gingo CMS</strong> is a GitHub organization for open-source content management projects.
We develop and maintain <strong>GingoCMS</strong> — a headless CMS you deploy on your own infrastructure.
</p>

<p>
Our goal is to give teams and independent developers a CMS without vendor lock-in:
you control the server, database, and data.
</p>

<h2 id="mission">Mission</h2>

<p>
Make headless CMS practical and accessible:
</p>

<ul>
<li><strong>Developer-first</strong> — a smooth experience for those integrating CMS into applications</li>
<li><strong>Editor-friendly</strong> — a clear interface for those who work with content</li>
<li><strong>Self-hosted by default</strong> — deploy on your own server, no mandatory SaaS</li>
<li><strong>Openness</strong> — transparent development and community participation</li>
</ul>
